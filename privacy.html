Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 23, 2026

CubeGym does not collect, store, or share any personal data.

Overview

CubeGym is a Rubik's cube training app designed with your privacy in mind. The app operates entirely offline and locally on your device.

Data Collection

We do not collect any data. Specifically:

  • No personal information is collected
  • No usage analytics or tracking
  • No third-party services or SDKs
  • No network requests are made
  • No advertising or ad tracking

Local Storage

All your solve times, statistics, and preferences are stored locally on your device using Apple's standard data storage. This data:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is not accessible to us or any third party
  • Is removed when you delete the app

Children's Privacy

CubeGym does not collect any data from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever change this policy, we will update this page and the "Last updated" date above.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, you can contact us at contact@cubegym.app.
